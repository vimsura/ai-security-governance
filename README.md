# AI Security & Governance

Practical notes and frameworks for securing AI systems
and governing their use in enterprise environments.

---

## Why This Repository Exists

AI systems are moving from decision-support tools to
autonomous agents. Traditional security and governance
models were not designed for this shift.

This repository documents **practical, security-first
approaches** to governing AI responsibly.

---

## Core Focus Areas

- AI identity and authorization
- Model access control and permissions
- Agentic AI risk management
- Governance frameworks and controls
- Auditability and compliance
- Enterprise and SAP-aligned security thinking
