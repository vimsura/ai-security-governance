# AI Risk Models

This section documents security and governance risk
models specific to AI and autonomous systems.

## Focus Areas
- Agentic AI risks
- Unauthorized autonomous actions
- Privilege escalation by AI agents
- Data leakage via model access
- Lack of auditability

## Goal
To help organizations identify, assess, and mitigate
AI-specific risks before they materialize.
